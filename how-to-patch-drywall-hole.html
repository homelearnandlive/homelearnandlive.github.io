This includes a putty knife, joint compound, sandpaper, and a paintbrush. It's also essential to clear the area around the hole of any furniture or flooring to prevent damage. Make sure to fill the hole completely, but avoid applying too much compound, as this can create a bulge in the ceiling. Allow the compound to dry completely before sanding it down to a smooth finish.
